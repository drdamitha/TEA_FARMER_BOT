About TeaAssist

TeaAssist is a domain-specific chatbot designed for tea farmers. It uses natural language processing to understand farmer questions and retrieves answers from a local knowledge base.

How It Works

1. Farmer asks a question
2. NLP engine detects intent
3. Database provides answer
4. Bot learns unknown answers
